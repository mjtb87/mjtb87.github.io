In Summary of what I Am...

My journey into the world of software development began with with a choice to take reality into my own hand in a way I never had before. After having some very heavy metaphysical realizations about what reality is and our inseparable place in it, I knew I had to throw myself into career that is as detailed and nuances as the reality that encompasses it. I can think of few other careers that spill over into almost every area of life as technology and software development. The capabilities of technology to rapidly change the world we live in cannot be denied.

We are at a pivotal point in history where our actions are so interconnected, the responsibility we have to ourselves and each other must be realized and actively acted upon. I have confidence and vision and I am actively seeking to find and work with people who have the same passion for life and discovery as I do. I know I'll be an integral part of any team I'm placed on. I want to be able to use my leadership and communication skills to inspire teams in an unparalleled way. I know ultimately all problems are systemic and breaking those viscous cycles and helping the movement of all parts through the spiral is a never ending process that I am proud to be part of.
